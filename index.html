About

Delphine’s recent experiences were at non-profit organizations as Dev Team Manager, Scrum Master, and Front Desk Manager. Her team describes her as someone who leads by example, team-oriented, and able to focus on both individuals and productivity. Delphine is enthusiastic, insightful, helps the team stay motivated, and is always ready to serve.

Delphine recently obtained a MEAN Stack Development Certificate from University of Missouri-Kansas City. With a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, she is highly numerate, has strong analytical and creative thinking skills. Her passion for learning is contagious: she possesses a Bachelor of Arts in French, Masters of Science in Administration with an emphasis in International Relations & some random certificates!

When she is not coding, she can be found adventuring with family & friends, volunteering in her community, or just reading a good book.
